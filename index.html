Finance & Capital

Lobby for startup capital, pool resource center, and access to funding beyond savings. Group savings and loaning schemes.

Why Women First?

As the founder, I am a woman — and women play a major role in the community, yet many lack connections to launch or sustain their projects.

We understand the challenging circumstances: inadequate services, illiteracy, poverty. Through knowledge, skills, and advocacy we can reduce poverty.

Our Guiding Principles

Vision

To be a leading self-reliant people, capable of affording basic needs and free from poverty, with improved standards of living.

Mission

To empower women with the means to achieve middle-income status and enable them become self‑reliant both socially and economically.
